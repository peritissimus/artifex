  • Infrastructure — Built platform using Google Colab and ngrok for rapid deployment and iteration
  • Global Recognition — Ranked in top 10 globally on pioneer.app for five consecutive months
  • Career Impact — Success led to invitation from Dubverse founder to join their founding team as an Individual Contributor
