SimpleSounds

Role: Co-Founder Period: January 2021 - December 2021

Overview

Co-founded SimpleSounds, a platform for generating high-fidelity AI voice-overs at 48kHz using neural voice technology. As Co-Founder, I spearheaded the technical development, building the infrastructure and AI pipeline from scratch.

Key Achievements

  • Built platform generating high-fidelity 48kHz Neural Voices
  • Developed 10+ distinct voice personas with unique characteristics
  • Achieved top MOS rating of 4.5 (Mean Opinion Score for voice quality)
  • Ranked in top 10 globally on pioneer.app for 5 consecutive months
  • Successfully raised attention from industry leaders

Technical Implementation

Built the entire technical stack using Python and Google Colab for model training and inference. Utilized ngrok for exposing services, creating a functional MVP that demonstrated the viability of high-quality AI voice generation.

Recognition

Featured on Product Hunt and maintained a consistent top ranking on pioneer.app, a platform for ambitious startups. The success of SimpleSounds led to an invitation from Dubverse's founder to join their founding team.

Technical Stack

Python AI/ML TTS Neural Networks Google Colab Audio Processing

Journey Forward

The experience and expertise gained from SimpleSounds directly contributed to my next role as Founding Engineer at Dubverse, where I helped build an AI video dubbing platform serving over 1 million users.
